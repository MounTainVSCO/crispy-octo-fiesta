Statement of Intent

I study how people interact with complex interfaces and design content systems that reduce confusion. My work sits at the intersection of design, writing, and cognitive load theory. Through mixed-methods research, I investigate how information architecture affects user comprehension and develop frameworks for creating more intuitive digital experiences. I'm particularly interested in how language choices in interfaces can either support or hinder users' mental models of complex systems.
