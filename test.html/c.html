A Bit More About Me

I've spent the last decade at the intersection of human psychology and emerging technology, fascinated by how we can build tools that make us more thoughtful rather than more frantic. My work spans from early-stage startups to organizations shaping the future of AI, always with a focus on creating sustainable, human-centered solutions.

When I'm not designing products or facilitating workshops, you'll find me reading philosophy, practicing meditation, or exploring the mountains around San Francisco. I believe that our best work comes from a place of clarity and intention—principles I try to bring to everything I create.

I'm particularly interested in the emergent behaviors that arise when humans and AI systems work together thoughtfully, and I spend a lot of time thinking about how we can design these collaborations to amplify our collective wisdom rather than our collective anxiety.
